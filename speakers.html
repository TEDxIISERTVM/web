Speaker Descriptions

Anica
Mann

A renowned historian with a passion for art, Anica Mann’s several interviews and appearances on podcasts are the best examples of her exemplary oratory skills. With a talk that is sure to enthrall and inform, we are very excited to hear more about her journey and experiences as an archaeologist and writer!




Dr. Tara
Rajendran

She heals, she writes, and she’s an instrumentalist! Words are not enough to describe her multi-faceted personality. Dr. Tara Rajendran is an oncologist, a short-story author, and a renowned Veena player. She is also the founder of the first physician-led music therapy advocacy program, 'Oncology and Strings’. Join us to listen to her talk about creativity, the arts, and much more!




Khyrunissa A.
 

Khyrunissa A, a writer from our childhood, is here to deliver the talk of a lifetime. Having had a diverse literary career, from Butterfingers to publishing multiple widely loved books like Tongue in Cheek: The Funny Side of Life and her most recent children’s collection, The Couch Potato Who Said Ouch and Other Funny Stories, she is truly a legend in her field. Join us to hear from a writer in such a way with words that you'll be begging for more.




Suresh
Eriyat

Suresh Eriyat, an animation maestro from Studio Eeksaurus, has not only woven magical tales through captivating visuals but has also been recognized for his exceptional talent with a National Award. His creative flair and industry insights make him your ideal companion on the animated adventure ahead, transforming aspirations into captivating narratives with an enchanting touch.




C.Anandharamakrishnan
 

You may have heard of the very cool concept of 3D food printing—you have Dr. C. Anandharamakrishnan to thank for his contribution to this emerging field of food technology and engineering. Previously Director of the National Institute of Food Technology, Entrepreneurship, and Management, Thanjavur (NIFTEM-T), Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, this expert is currently the Director of CSIR – National Institute of Interdisciplinary Science and Technology (NIIST), Thiruvananthapuram.




Anwesh
Kumar Das

With a fascinating history of working in the oil and energy industry, Anwesh Kumar Das is here to take you through his journey of working in the oil industry and how he rose to the position of Deputy Chief Production Engineer at Oil India Limited. Join us at TEDxIISERTVM to hear about this inspirational visionary!




Susan
Thomas

Susan Thomas, an alumna of JNU and Oxford, is a woman with many accomplishments associated with her name as an IRS officer who has also worked with the Ministry of Textiles and as a compelling public speaker and debater. On Instagram, she shares a diverse range of content, offering advice on topics spanning from fashion to navigating the complexities of UPSC exams. In essence, she is someone for youngsters to look up to. Adding to the allure, her captivating collection of sarees is the perfect finishing touch to her multifaceted persona.
